Meet Us

Creekside Logistics is a value-driven logistics service provider incorporated in November 2018, located in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. We are commited to ensuring individuals, business owners and government institutions measure up their logistics operations with the fast, ever-changing world. We guarantee customers remain relevant in a highly competive business environment by giving them a seamless experience with fast deliveries.
